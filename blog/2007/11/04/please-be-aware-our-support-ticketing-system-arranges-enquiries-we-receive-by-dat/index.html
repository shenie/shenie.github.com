Please be aware our support ticketing system arranges enquiries we receive by date. We respond to the oldest enquiries first. Each new response you submit, updates the date of your ticket. To ensure you receive a response as quickly as possible, please do not reply to your original support request until you have received a response from our staff. We also request that our customers do not submit multiple support requests to avoid unnecessary confusion.
